Yasmin Shenair - Web Developer

I have a passion for creating elegant, user-friendly applications. I recently completed a comprehensive program at iO Academy, where I gained hands-on experience with a range of programming languages and frameworks including Javascript, PHP, HTML, CSS, React, OOP, and Slim 4.

During my time at iO Academy, I had the opportunity to work on a variety of exciting projects, both independently and as part of a team. I also developed a deep understanding of Object-Oriented Programming concepts, and was able to apply these principles to create efficient and maintainable code.

In addition to my technical skills, I am a dedicated learner and enjoy keeping up with the latest trends and technologies in the software development field. I believe that by continually expanding my knowledge and skills, I can create even more impactful and innovative applications that truly make a difference.
